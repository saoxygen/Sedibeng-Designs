ABOUT US

Who Are we?

I'm Sedibeng, the creative force and owner of Ko Sedibeng Designs. Our journey officially began in 2022, and we proudly call Sebokeng in the scenic Vaal area, Johannesburg, our home. My fascination with unique earrings and accessories has always led me to market stalls and street shops, seeking one-of-a-kind pieces. When one of my treasured earrings broke, I decided to get crafty. I took its findings and combined them with vibrant beads to create a new earring. That's when the spark for my brand ignited.

Crafting earrings soon became more than a hobby; it became a therapeutic passion. From this humble beginning, we've evolved to offer not just earrings but also bracelets, catering to both male and female customers. Our bracelets stand out with personalized charms that add a unique touch. As we look to the future, our vision extends beyond accessories. We aspire to venture into clothing and handbags, expanding our brand's horizons. The journey is just beginning, and we're excited about the growth and possibilities ahead.

Our Mission

Ko Sedibeng Designs, we strive to elevate personal expression through unique handcrafted pieces that tell a story. While commiting to quality, craftsmanship, innovative designs, and providing affordable pieces.

Creating a brand that encourages individuality, celebrates diverse occasions, and to become a cherished part of your life.
